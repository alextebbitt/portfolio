About me

I was born in 1991 in southern England. I have been living in Spain for 9 years now. When I was 8 years old I started racing karts. My main interests are languages, Cricket, windsurfing and music.

After giving up karting because it was too expensive I was encouraged to find a different hobby. I chose Cricket and Music. I learnt the drums, then a few years later I started learning the guitar and bass. I was very interested in songwriting. This lead me to create a homestudio in my bedroom. I have an album out on spotify. Spotify Alex Tebbitt. As shown in the homepage I play the drums and I have a band here in Valencia. We should be playing on the 18th of June in a liverpool bar Russafa Valencia. Here are a few more videos.

Together with learning Music I also took up Cricket. I managed to play at a high level when I was between the ages of 15 to 18. I was the captain of my youth cricket team. This was a great experience and taught me how to lead and manage a team.

Now I am studying at the Bridge. In a full stack intensive programming course. I hope to learn many things and prepare myself to obtain a job in this industy and to continue growing as a professional software developer.
